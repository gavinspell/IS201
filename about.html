Our founding

Padanaram Diving Company was started by Alex Barrachina as a one-man hull cleaning service. We have quickly grown and expanded our services to include detailing and countless other diving services. Our team is up for any job you can throw at us.

Growth & beyond

In recent news, the ownership of Padanaram Diving Company has been transferred to Gavin Spell. Gavin was raised in Dartmouth, spending his childhood years out on the water in various sailing programs and working around Padanaram. When not SCUBA diving, Gavin can be found driving launch at New Bedford Yacht club or boating with his family and friends.
